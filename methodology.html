Our Methodology & Advertising Disclosure

Last reviewed July 2026

How we earn money

The Ledger is supported by affiliate commissions. When you click certain links on this site and go on to register with an operator, we may earn a referral fee. This does not cost you anything extra, and it does not influence which operators we list, how we rank them, or what we say about them in a review. We do not accept payment in exchange for a favorable review, a higher ranking, or removal of a negative point.

Who we list — and who we don't

We only list and link to online casinos currently licensed by the New Jersey Division of Gaming Enforcement (DGE) and legally operating in New Jersey. Before adding or renewing any listing, we check the operator against the DGE's official list of Internet Gaming Authorized Sites. We do not list offshore or unregulated operators under any circumstances, regardless of any commission they may offer.

What our ratings do and don't mean

Our ratings reflect our own editorial assessment of licensing status, bonus transparency, game library, and payout reliability at the time of review. They are not a guarantee of any outcome from gambling, which always carries financial risk regardless of which operator you choose. No bonus advertised on this site is risk-free or guaranteed to result in winnings.

Advertising standards we hold ourselves to

In line with New Jersey's advertising rules for regulated gaming, we do not use language suggesting a bet or bonus is "risk-free" or that winnings are guaranteed, we do not misrepresent wagering requirements, and every page carrying gambling advertising displays the 1-800-GAMBLER helpline clearly. If you believe any page on this site falls short of that standard, please contact us and we will correct it.
