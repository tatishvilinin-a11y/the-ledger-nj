Welcome bonus: why the offer looks smaller than it used to

Golden Nugget refreshed its NJ welcome package in April 2026, and the change is worth flagging explicitly because a lot of third-party bonus-code sites still describe the old version. Previously, new players got 500 Flex Spins plus a 24-hour "lossback" — a refund of 100% of net losses on eligible games up to $1,000, paid as Casino Credits. As of this update, the lossback has been retired. The current offer is 500 Flex Spins only, usable across 100+ eligible slot games, triggered by a minimum $5 deposit with no promo code required. Spins are issued in batches of 50 per day over 10 days, are non-withdrawable, and each expires 24 hours after you select the game you want to play it on — worth explaining clearly, since "500 spins" reads very differently once a reader understands they can't just fire off all 500 in one sitting.

In practical terms: this is now a smaller welcome offer than BetMGM's or Caesars' straight deposit matches, and meaningfully smaller than what Golden Nugget itself offered a few months ago. Winnings from the spins carry only a 1x playthrough requirement, which is player-friendly, but any separately claimed deposit bonus funds still carry a 15x wagering requirement — a distinction readers comparing operators on "wagering requirement" alone can easily miss.
