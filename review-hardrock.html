Welcome bonus: how a "lossback" offer differs from a deposit match

When that 24-hour window closes, Hard Rock Bet credits you a Casino Bonus equal to 100% of your net cash losses during that window, up to $1,000, delivered within about 30 minutes of the window closing. In plain terms: this isn't free money up front like a deposit match. It's downside protection — if you lose money on slots in your first day of real play, you get a large chunk of it back as bonus credit. If you don't lose, you simply keep your winnings and never trigger the lossback at all. That's a meaningfully different value proposition than BetMGM's or Caesars' upfront match bonuses, and it's worth explaining to readers rather than just listing "$1,000 bonus" alongside the others as if it works the same way.
